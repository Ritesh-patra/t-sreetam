About Dr. Seetam Kumar

Dr. T. Seetam Kumar is a gold-medalist interventional radiologist with specialist expertise in neurovascular and endovascular interventions. He manages complex cerebrovascular and systemic vascular conditions using minimally invasive, image-guided techniques.

From acute stroke interventions to tumor embolisation and advanced pediatric vascular procedures, Dr. Seetam combines strong diagnostic radiology training with advanced fellowship training to provide precise, evidence-based care.

Our Medical Specializations

Advanced interventional procedures with precision and expertise

Acute Stroke Interventions

Emergency endovascular procedures for acute stroke treatment, including mechanical thrombectomy and clot retrieval.

Aneurysm Treatment

Minimally invasive coiling and stenting procedures for cerebral and peripheral aneurysms with advanced techniques.

Tumor Embolisation

Targeted embolization therapy to reduce tumor blood supply and enhance treatment outcomes for various malignancies.

Pediatric Vascular Procedures

Specialized interventional procedures for children, including congenital vascular malformations and pediatric angioplasty.
